User Manual

📸 How to Play

Click the glowing camera button on the home screen. Take a photo of your trash or eco-items. Our local AI will analyze its environmental impact and give a score from 1-10.

🌱 Leveling & Evolution

If you responsibly recycle/dispose of the item, your monster gains XP equal to the eco-score. Your monster evolves and grows larger at Levels 10, 20, 30, 40, and unlocks its ultimate form at Level 50!

🛍️ Monster Mall

Use your earned XP to buy cute outfits and accessories in the Shop. You can equip them in the Backpack tab.

🏆 Eco Certificates

Check your History tab. Reaching milestones (Lv 5, 10, 20, 30, 40, 50) unlocks exclusive Eco Certificates to prove your dedication to the planet!
